Overview

Click is a money transfer app that enables users to send money to over 8 middle east countries with Russian rubbles in a very easy few clicks. Students who study at Russian universities can also pay their university expenses.

Users also can exchange these 8 countries’ currencies for the russian ruble using google based money exchange feature.

The app contains more than 30+ pages.
